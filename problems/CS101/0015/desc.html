This program will convert degrees Fahrenheit to degrees Celsius, and convert it back. You will write just the functions f2c and c2f in your file and submit it, and the server will test the functions with several values. The formula for Celsius to Fahrenheit is to take the degrees, multiply by 5, divide by 9, and add 32. The forumla for Fahrenheit to Celsius is to take the degrees, subtract 32, then multiply by 5 and divide by 9. You will have to download the file degrees.py to test your functions.
